        Frequently asked questions

        • How much can I order online (Min/Max)?

          Click & Collect orders for Travel Money or Asda Travel Money Card have a minimum order value of £100 and maximum of £2,500 per person per day.

          Home delivery orders for Travel Money or Asda Travel Money Card have a minimum order value of £350 and maximum of £2,500 per person per day.

          The minimum load for Asda Travel Money Card is £50, however this is currently only available at an Asda Travel Money Bureau.

        • What ID do I require?

          When collecting your online order from an Asda store, you need to bring:

          - The payment card you used to place the order.

          - Valid photo ID - either a valid passport or full UK photographic driving licence, which matches the cardholder’s details. We do not accept provisional driving licences.

          For home delivery orders, a signature will be required on delivery.

        • Are there any charges for using my credit or debit card?

          You will not be charged a card processing fee by us if you choose to pay by debit or credit card. However, your card issuer may apply a fee so you should check with them before you buy your travel money. Any fee applied by your card issuer will not appear on your travel money order, but will appear on your card statement.

        • What are the delivery charges?
          • If you’re collecting your order from an Asda Travel Money Bureau then collection will be free.
          • If you’re collecting your order from an Asda store that doesn’t have a Travel Money Bureau then there will be £4.99 delivery fee for all orders under £400.
          • Home delivery is free on all orders of £500 or more. A £4.99 delivery charge is applied to all home delivery orders between £350 and £499.99.
        • When will I receive my order?

          For Click and Collect orders of, where the Asda store has an Asda Travel Money bureau, the earliest day you can collect your order is:

          Order confirmed before 2 pm.
          Collect at specified location after 3pm, on day of collection.

          When you're collecting from a Customer Service desk, the earliest date for collection will be automatically displayed when selecting your preferred collection point. Please note some ASDA stores may not be included in the list of preferred collection points. Your collection date can also be found after you’ve completed your transaction in your Travel Money order email confirmation.

          For home delivery orders, the earliest day we can deliver your order is shown below. Please note:

          - All home delivery orders are sent by Royal Mail Special Delivery (unless we advise you otherwise) and only to your credit or debit card billing address.

          - Orders requested for Monday delivery, can sometimes be delivered on the Saturday before. On Bank Holidays, where a delivery was requested for Tuesday, you may receive your delivery on the Saturday before.

        • Can I cancel my order?

          You can cancel an order if you have chosen to collect it from an Asda Travel Money bureau. Please make sure you contact us 24 hours in advance of the collection time, otherwise you will be charged a £15 late cancellation fee. To cancel, please contact our Customer Service team.

          Cancelled orders will be refunded with the full sterling amount that you paid for your Travel Money order.

          If you have placed an order home delivery, or collection at an Asda store that does not have a Travel Money bureau, this cannot be cancelled or amended once your order has been confirmed by us.

        • What is ASDA Rewards pricing?

          The ASDA Rewards prices will be better than the standard foreign exchange rate advertised online on the date of purchase.

          ASDA Rewards prices are only available on the sell rates of currencies in stock on the date of your online purchase.

          When purchasing online you must enter a valid ASDA Rewards number to obtain the ASDA Rewards better rate.

        • What happens if my online order is unsuccessful?

          You may be offered our pay on collection service. With pay on collection you can take advantage of our online rates by selecting a date and location to collect your order, with payment not being taken until you collect your order. You will receive the rates you would have received had your initial online order been successful.

        • Why can’t I find the store I’m looking for?

          Pay on collection is only available where we have an Asda Travel Money store as you’ll need to pay for your order when you collect it. If you previously chose collection from a Customer Service Desk this will be unavailable as a collection point.

        • How do I pay using Apple Pay?

          In addition to using your credit and debit card, you can complete your online purchase using Apple Pay if the card issuer you’re using is Maestro, Mastercard or VISA. Your credit card issuer will likely charge you a cash advance fee and interest from the time of purchase with us.

          1. Tap the Apple Pay button or choose Apple Pay as your payment method.

          2. To pay with a different card, tap the Next button or the Expand Menu button next to your default card.

          3. If necessary, enter your billing, delivery and contact information. Apple Pay stores that information so you won't need to enter it again.

          4. Confirm the payment. iPhone or iPad with Face ID: Double-click the side button, then use Face ID or your passcode.

          iPhone or iPad without Face ID: Use Touch ID or your passcode.

          Mac with Touch ID: Follow the prompts on the Touch Bar and place your finger on Touch ID. If Touch ID is off, tap the Apple Pay icon on the Touch Bar and follow the prompts on the screen.

          Mac without Touch ID: Confirm the payment on your Bluetooth-connected iPhone or Apple Watch. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID on all devices.

          When your payment is successful, you'll see ‘Done’ and a tick on the screen.

        • How do I pay using Google Pay?

          In addition to using your credit and debit card, you can complete your online purchase using Google Pay if the card issuer you’re using is Mastercard or VISA. Your credit card issuer will likely charge you a cash advance fee and interest from the time of purchase with us.

          1. Tap the Google Pay button to choose Google Pay as your payment method.

          2. To pay with a different card or add a new card, tap the expand menu button next to your default card.

          3. Confirm the payment using your Google biometrics or your passcode to authorise payment.
